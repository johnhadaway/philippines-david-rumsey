Note	Pictorial map of the Philippine Islands, on sheet 44x41. Showing the archipelago highlighted on a deep blue ground within a circular porthole. Surrounding this are details of Japan, China, Indo-China, Sumatra, Java, Bali, Australia and New Guinea. Includes many brightly colored vignettes of native costumes,activities, wildlife, and places of interest depicted throughout the map. Relief shown pictorially. Includes legend: "All distances are from Manila. There are 7,083 Philippine Islands. 466 are over one square mile in area." On verso text and views of Picturesque Manila; Birds-eye-view of Manila from U.S. Army Plane. and Manila Hotel.
